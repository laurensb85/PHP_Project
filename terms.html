The current agreement aims to document the transfer of rights and payment with respect to the exploitation of the work of the licensor, as defined in art. 3 of this agreement.

The Licensor grants the Licensee a limited non-exclusive license regarding the exploitation rights listed below, associated with the work that is described in Art. 3 of this agreement:

- The work of the licensor will be used in the construction of a database of pictures which may exclusively be used for scientific purposes.

o Phase 1: In the first phase of the study, the photos will be offered digitally to participants and they will be assessed on their emotional characteristics. This presentation of photos will occur via PC or projection in a test room. The subjects are hereby not allowed to reproduce the images for personal or other use. In short, there will be a digital reproduction (via Internet) of the work of the licensor.

o Phase 2: In a second phase, the work of the licensor will be made available for scientific use by other researchers. By downloading the work, the user must explicitly agree to a clause that the photos will be used only for scientific purposes.

included, are property of the licensor.

The granting of a limited non-exclusive license, referred to in art. 2 of this agreement, refers to pictures which the licensor chooses to share. The names of these uploaded photos will be stored in a database and can always be found in the confirmation email.
The participant declares that the uploaded photos are his / her property or that he / she has permission from the actual owner to share them by uploading.

The license described in art. 2, is granted for a period of unlimited duration.

The licence described in art. 2, is granted for use all over the world.

In using the work of the licensor (see Art. 2) a mention of the licensors name on the photographs is not possible because of scientific reasons.
